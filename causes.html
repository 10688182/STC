You want to become a member?

How to become a member.

Any member desiring to become a member shall complete a proposal form which shall be supported by signatories of two (2) members of good standing as referees. The proposal form shall attract a non refundable fee to be determined by executives. The name of the person desiring to become a member of the club shall be posted on the notice board for a period of one (1) month. Objections thereof shall be lodged with the secretary within one (1) month.

Any person shall be eligible for full membership provided that they are sixteen years of age or over. Children below the age of sixteen may be elected as Junior Members without the right to vote or hold office.
